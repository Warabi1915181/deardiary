# Dear Diary

A private relationship companion for two people — part diary, part memory box, part gentle planner. This context covers the design language the app is built in.

## Language

### Scenes

**Scene**:
One of the app's two complete emotional renderings. A scene is a mood with its own palette, not a brightness setting.
_Avoid_: Mode, theme, appearance

**Morning**:
The light scene — a soft morning scrapbook of warm cream, blush pink, and soft rose.
_Avoid_: Light mode, light theme

**Candlelight**:
The dark scene — a genuinely dark, warm-lit private room. Its own mood, never Morning dimmed.
_Avoid_: Dark mode, dark theme

**Enclosure**:
What "private" means visually: being inside a small warm-lit room with the door closed. Candlelight expresses privacy through cozy dimness.

**Warmth Bridge**:
The rule that unifies the two scenes: every color in both scenes is warm-tinted. No neutral gray, no pure black, no cool tint anywhere.

### Color roles

**Romance Accent**:
The single accent role carrying the app's emotional emphasis. It renders as rose in Morning and as Ember in Candlelight.
_Avoid_: Primary color, brand color, tint color

**Ember**:
The candle-flame / sunset-orange hue family that Romance Accent renders as in Candlelight. Night-only; it never appears in Morning.

**Heart Rose**:
The muted dusty-rose reserved in Candlelight for the heart glyph itself (`heart`, `heart.fill`) and nothing else. A container holding a heart glyph is still Ember.

**Backdrop**:
The room — the outermost background a screen sits on.

**Surface**:
The paper — the fill of cards, sheets, and popovers. In Candlelight, higher surfaces are a step lighter, as if catching the glow.
_Avoid_: Card color, white

**Surface Muted**:
A recessed variant of Surface for wells: photo placeholders, control tracks, inset panels.
_Avoid_: Muted

**Ink**:
Primary text color.

**Ink Muted**:
Secondary text color for metadata and quiet labels.

**Sage**:
The green support role (done, growth). Renders as sage green in Morning and shifts olive-ward, desaturated, in Candlelight.

**Plum**:
The purple support role (secondary flavor). Renders as muted lavender in Morning and shifts plum-ward, desaturated, in Candlelight.
_Avoid_: Lavender (as a role name)
